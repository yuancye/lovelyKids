Background Story

Inspired by parental concerns, I observed a lack of interest and discipline in my children's daily routinesâ€”skipping bedtime and morning routines, untidy rooms, procrastination, and an excessive focus on treats.

In an effort to instill positive behaviors and foster good habits, we introduced an 'Earn-Punishment' system we affectionately call the 'Rule of Living.'

Our initial implementation of the 'Rule of Living' showed promise in motivating our kids to adopt better behaviors. However, the manual recording of earned stars or deductions proved challenging to sustain. This led to a decline in its effectiveness over time.

With the creation of this web application, we aim to streamline the recording process, making it easier to track and maintain consistency in rewarding positive behaviors and addressing negative ones. Our hope is that this digital tool will not only prolong the effectiveness of the 'Rule of Living' but also facilitate the formation of lasting, positive habits in our children's lives."
